When enabled, customers and jobs created or updated in RentalCore will automatically be synchronized to your Twenty CRM instance.

Base URL of your Twenty instance, e.g. https://app.twenty.com
Found in Twenty under Settings → API & Webhooks → API Keys.

How It Works

Customers

Customers with a company name are synced as Companies in Twenty. Individual customers (first/last name only) are synced as People.

Jobs

Jobs are synced as Opportunities in Twenty. The job status is mapped to a Twenty pipeline stage and the revenue is stored as the opportunity amount.

Sync direction

Sync is one-way from RentalCore to Twenty. Changes made directly in Twenty are not reflected in RentalCore. Syncs happen automatically in the background when records are saved.
