Live workflows

Keep show days calm with these day-to-day routines crafted alongside the warehouse floor.

  • Job planning: create the job, add packages, reserve crew, and lock status “Prep”.
  • Pack validation: use the scan board for live pack progress, trigger alerts for missing gear.
  • Rental reconciliation: capture rental equipment returns, flag damages, attach photos directly from the job detail.

Rapid fixes

Most issues are solved within minutes—start with these diagnostic shortcuts.

  • Scanner stuck offline? Run ./restart-dev.sh then re-authenticate via Passkey.
  • Device unavailable? Open Devices → Availability and clear finished jobs.
  • Exports failing? Check archive permissions, then rerun the export via Analytics.
